# Aerivo (calmatropium) — HCP reference (fictional)

Class: Long-acting muscarinic antagonist (LAMA), inhaled bronchodilator.
Manufacturer: Norwick Pharma.

## Mechanism of action
Calmatropium is a long-acting antagonist at muscarinic receptors (M3 in bronchial
smooth muscle), producing sustained bronchodilation with once-daily dosing.

## Approved indications
- Chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD): maintenance bronchodilation.
- Asthma: add-on maintenance in specified patient populations.

## Key safety notes for HCPs
- Anticholinergic effects: dry mouth is common; caution in narrow-angle glaucoma,
  urinary retention, prostatic hyperplasia.
- Not a rescue inhaler — not for acute bronchospasm.
- Two delivery devices exist (dry-powder inhaler, soft-mist inhaler);
  confirm patient inhaler technique.

## Typical HCP question triggers
Device selection and technique, LAMA/LABA combination therapy, positioning
within COPD/asthma treatment guidelines.
